# Thera Research Product Context

## Register

brand

## Product Purpose

Thera Research is a Chile-based Clinical Research Organization website for sponsors, clinical teams, investigators, and patients. The site must communicate local ownership, clinical rigor, regulatory coordination, monitoring capability, patient recruitment, and current company activity.

## Primary Users

- Sponsors and study leads evaluating clinical execution support in Chile.
- Pharmaceutical, biotechnology, generic drug, OTC/consumer healthcare, and medical device teams.
- Investigators and site stakeholders who need a credible operational partner.
- Patients interested in registering for study recruitment follow-up.

## Brand Voice

Calm, clinical, precise, responsible. The site should feel like a high-trust scientific operations firm, not a generic healthcare startup or a hospital brochure.

## Visual Thesis

A premium clinical intelligence surface: dark cobalts, disciplined neutrals, emerald scientific signal, restrained photography, precise spacing, and motion that feels measured.

## Content Principles

- Lead with responsibility, local execution, and study continuity.
- Keep copy scan-friendly in both English and Spanish.
- Avoid repeating the same positioning line across sections.
- Separate sponsor workflows from patient recruitment workflows.
- Treat LinkedIn activity as a newsroom, with original post links preserved.

## Anti-References

- Generic white-and-teal healthcare landing pages.
- SaaS card grids with icons above every heading.
- Oversized hero typography that compromises legibility.
- Decorative medical stock imagery that does not explain operations.
- Animation that calls attention to itself instead of improving hierarchy.

## Conversion Priorities

1. Sponsors can quickly understand what Thera Research does.
2. Patients can register interest without confusion.
3. Visitors can verify current company activity through LinkedIn.
4. Contact and LinkedIn actions remain visible and credible across the site.
